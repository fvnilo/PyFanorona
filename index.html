Welcome to PyFanorona

This project is a simple implementation of the Fanorona board game in Python. The rules of the game are simple:

  • The game is played on a 9x5 board;
  • White starts;
  • Each player alternate turns;
  • Possible moves are limited and defined by the lines on a board;
  • To win, you have to capture all of your opponent's pieces;
  • There are two kinds of capturing move: approaching one of your piece to an adjacent space of an enemy piece or withdrawing your piece away from an adjacent space;
  • Every enemy piece that was in the line of action is captured until the line is interrupted (a blank space of own piece);
  • Capturing moves are obligatory if any is possible;
  • Just like checkers, combos are allowed until no capture is possible

What you need

  • Python 2.7

Status

Very minimalistic text-based interface. Need to eventually have a GUI. Combos are also not implemented yet.
